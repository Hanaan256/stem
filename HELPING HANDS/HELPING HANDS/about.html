CLUB HISTORY

The light academy helping hands club is a charity-oriented club engaging students in charity, welfare and brotherhood. It therefore carries out all charity activities on behalf of the school and students’ fraternity. All activities of 3c, red cross and rotary. With the main objective being of significant help to our community. All this done under school authorization and supervision. The was founded in 2017 by two young vibrant students by the names of Noor Awes Mohammed and Philemon Ojuman. It has since been handed down to younger and more vibrant students that have shown great leadership skills to the lead to great achievements.

“When we give cheerfully and accept gratefully, everyone is blessed.”

Mission & Vision

The Helping Hands Club at Light Academy Secondary School is making a positive impact on the community by collecting new or gently used shoes and clothing, money, stationery material, soap and other items used in our daily life that will be donated. The club's founder Sheik Habib started the organization with a mission "to help the most vulnerable members of communities”. The club has grown to 157 members in more than 7 years, with the intention of creating a better future for their club before passing it down to the next class. The organization has taken on virtual projects and encouraged youth leadership. The first initiative of the club was to enable different students to learn that in life people are not the same. There are the needy people and other vulnerable groups of people. The club came up to enable students to have a sense of well-being.
